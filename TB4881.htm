Singapore: Buildings Plan Approved

Source: The Property Market Information, Urban Redevelopment Authority

Definition(s)

Building Plan Approval
Building Plan Approval refers to the approval granted by the Commissioner of Building Control in respect of building plans and specifications submitted in accordance with the prescribed building regulations in force.

Residential Buildings
Residential Buildings refer to a building or a contiguous space in that part of a building for dwelling purposes. Residential units are categorised into landed (comprising detached houses, semi-detached houses and terrace houses) and non-landed (comprising apartments and condominiums). Cluster houses, townhouses, strata detached and strata semi-detached housing are categorised as detached, semi-detached or terrace houses depending on their building forms. Apartments include HUDC units which have not been privatised, apartments above shops and privatised residential units previously under the Government Housing Scheme for employees. Hostels, HDB flats, tenement houses, parsonages are excluded.

Commercial Properties
Commercial Properties refer to office space, shop space and hotel rooms.

Industrial Properties
Industrial Properties refer to factory space and warehouse space.

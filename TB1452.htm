Indonesia: Trade Statistics: Carry Over System

Source: Indonesia Statistic Bureau (BPS)


Methodology

Foreign trade consist of export and import on goods. All data compiled are based on documents of export-import declaration issued by Custom and Excise Offices. Data obtained are based on complete enumeration, and received from Custom and Excise Offices (Kantor Pelayanan Bea dan Cukai) located across the country.

Export refers to Free On Board (FOB) value. Export statistics are based on General Trade System which covers all Indonesian geographical areas. Country of destination is the country known at the time of dispatch to be the final country where goods are to be delivered.

Import refers to Cost Insurance and Freight (CIF) value. Import statistics are based on Special Trade System which covers all Indonesian geographical areas excluding Free Trade Zone which is treated as Foreign trade. Country of origin is the country in which the goods have been produced, after being verified by the Custom and Excise Offices, pursuant to the prevailing regulations.

Both exports and imports are stated in US Dollars, except in the data tables on quantity measurements, where they will be stated in net weight Kilograms.

All goods are included in the exports and imports data, except the following:
- Clothes and jewelry of passengers.
- Luggage of passegers of own use, except refrigerators, television sets, etc.
- Goods exported/imported for the use of countries representative (embassies).
- Goods for expeditions and exhibition shows.
- A part of the goods directly exported/imported by armed forces.
- Packing containers to be refilled.
- Bank notes and securities.
- Sample goods.


Notes & Remarks

The statistics on export and import values is the Balance of Payments are different from the statistics of export and import values compiled by Central Bureau of Statistics because of following reasons:
- Differences in the concept used for recording data.
- The import statistics of the Central Bureau of Statistics are derived only from PIUD documents. Import statistics derived from non-PIUD documents are not recorded by the Central Bureau of Statistics.

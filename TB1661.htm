Methodology

Definition(s)

Volume of Shares Traded
The Volume of Shares Traded is the volume of daily transaction at the Indonesia stock exchange.

Average Volume of Shares Traded
The Average Volume of Shares Traded is the total number of shares traded divided by the number of transaction days (Monday through Friday except holiday).

Trading Volume, Value and Frequency
Figures of Trading Volume, Value and Frequency are calculated once, except on the JSX' members trading data. Trading Frequency refers to number of transaction.

Number of Shares (Number of Issued Shares)
This figure is number of listed shares after adjustment when bonus and right issues, share splits and consolidations are made.

Number of Listed Shares
This figure refers to the total number of shares that is already listed and can be traded at the exchange.

Foreign Ownership
In September 1997, the foreign ownership limitation was abolished except for banks (49% of total listed shares). In May 1999, the maximum number of listed share and foreign ownership on banks company was set to 99%, based on Government regulation dated May 7th, 1999. JSX calculate this figure by adding net buying/selling by foreign investor for each stock to the previous figure.

Scope

Sea transport statistics comes from all Sea port in Indonesia, both commercial and non-commercial.

 

Definition(s)

Cargo Loaded

Cargo Loaded is the number of cargo loaded into ships leaving for domestic or foreign seaport.

 

Cargo Unloaded

Cargo Unloaded is the number of cargo unloaded out of ships coming from domestic or foreign seaport.

 

National

National is the local or Indonesian flag the ship is carrying.

 

Foreign

Foreign is the other country�s flag the ship is carrying.

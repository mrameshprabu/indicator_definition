Ukraine: Industrial Production Index

Source: State Statistics Service of Ukraine

Background

The industrial production index covers mining, manufacturing, production and distribution of electricity. The sort-term reporting covers about 90% of production enterprises and the general volume of products sold by industry (excluding products sold by enterprises (clients). The index depends on the country�s economic structure. The index is compiled monthly on the basis of industrial output valued at comparable prices.

 

Scope

The methodology for index compilation is in line with international standards - International Recommendations for the Index of Industrial Production, UN, 2009 edition.  It is based on the usage of data on dynamics of products output according to a fixed set of representative commodities (about 1000 headlines).

Values of production for compilation of the index are registered in enterprises and local units by industry and territory. Data are collected at enterprises and submitted by local statistical departments/divisions.

 

Methodology

Index is produced by types of activity at 3-4 digit level of the national classification by types of economic activity (CTEA) harmonized with NACE (Rev. 1.1- 2002). Products are classified according to the Nomenclature of Industrial Products (NIP) based on CPA 2002.

Index is produced on the basis of data from enterprises about monthly output of representative goods using Laspereys formula. Gross value added by types of activity for the base year 2007=100% is used as weights. On the basis of indices over average monthly value for the base year, the indices over the previous month, relevant month and the period of the previous year are produced. Data are published without amendments for seasonality factor.

According to NACE, industrial production refers to economic grouping, which consists of businesses whose activities fall under sections C, D and E (Mining, Manufacturing and Electricity, Gas & Water).

Basic information for constructing the index is the data on production of goods in physical terms without attempts to determine consumption of raw materials. Definition(s)

Industrial Production Index
Weighted average data on distribution of gross value added by the industrial type of activity from the relevant individual indices for each product.

 

Release Schedule

Data on industrial production index are released monthly, within 3 weeks after the end of the reference month.

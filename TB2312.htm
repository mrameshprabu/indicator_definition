Vietnam: Freight Carried

Source: General Statistics Office

Background

Freight Carried reflects the results of goods transportation of all types of economic activities in a given period of time; it is the basis for analyzing and evaluating goods transportation in the period. Also, it serves managing and making plans for goods transportation.

 

Definition(s)

Freight Carried

Freight Carried refers the total volume of cargoes transported by transportation establishments and others operating in transportation business activities, regardless of the traveling distance.

 

Methodology

Volume of freight carried is calculated by real weight of freight carried (including wrapping, if any). The volume of freight carried is only calculated after the transportation finishes, the goods is delivered to the destination as stated in the transport contract, and all procedures for loading goods in calculation means have finished; for bulky goods transported by cars, in case the direct calculation cannot be done, the volume of goods carried is calculated by 50% of weight ton of the means of transport, or by agreement between clients and transporters.

 

Release Schedule

Freight Carried is compiled and released in monthly and annual basis.

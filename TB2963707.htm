Definition(s)

Capacity Utilization is a production indicator of the manufacturing sector by comparing the actual production level with the corresponding full capacity level. This reflects the capability to accommodate expansion in production, exports and domestic price pressure.

Release Schedule

The production value index is compiled and released at monthly interval. Preliminary monthly production value index are released a month after the end of the reference month. The revised figures are released a month later together with the preliminary figures for the next reference month. The statistics are announced through press release to the public and are also posted on the Office of Industrial Economics website on the same day.

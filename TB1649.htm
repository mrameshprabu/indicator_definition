Vietnam: Foreign Direct Investment

Source: General Statistics Office

Background

Foreign Direct Investment indicator provides managers and policy makers with timely information on quantity of projects, capital which is registered and licensed in the period of study (month, quarter, year) and added capital of projects which are ongoing to evaluate attraction of investment of the economy.

 

Definition(s)

Foreign Direct Investment
Foreign Direct Investment means the brining of capital into Vietnam in the form of money or any assets by foreign investors for the purpose of carrying on investment activities in accordance with the provisions of the Law on foreign investment in Vietnam.

Invested (Registered) Capital
Invested (Registered) Capital means the capital required to implement an investment project, including legal capital and loan capital.

Legal Capital
Legal Capital of an enterprise with foreign owned capital means the capital required to establish the enterprise as stated in its charter.

 

Notes and Remarks

1)       Capital contribution of foreign parties to the legal capital of a joint venture enterprise shall be agreed by the parties and shall not be limited provided that the contribution is not less than 30 percent of the legal capital, except in cases stipulated by the Government.

2)       The legal capital of an enterprise with foreign owned capital must be a least thirty percent of its invested capital. In special cases and subject to approval of the body in charge of State management of foreign investment, this proportion may be lower than 30 percent.

3)       FDI under Investment (44471801; VOCJBA) refers to ytd monthly data of Registered Capital of Newly Licensed Projects which get the approval investment certificates from the government in reporting period.

4)       The ytd December data (44471801; VOCJBA) is released in reporting month without further revision as source’s policy. Meanwhile, annual data of series Foreign Direct Investment: Registered Capital (162654901, VODBBA) is released one year later.

 

Release Schedule

Foreign Direct Investment is published in monthly, quarterly and annual basis.

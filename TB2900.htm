Background

Source: Bursa Malaysia

Definition(s)

Exchange Main Board Share (EMAS) Index
Exchange Main Board Share (EMAS) Index refers to the share price performance of all Main Board companies.

Kuala Lumpur Composite Index (CI)
Kuala Lumpur Composite Index(KLCI) refers to the share price performance of the 100 component stocks on the Bursa Malaysia's Main Board.

Technology Index
The Technology Index is an all share index comprising both Main Board and Second Board Technology Sector stocks.

Syariah Index
The Syariah Index is a weighted average index with its components comprising the securities of Main Board companies, which have been designated as Syariah Approved Securities by the Syariah Advisory Council (SAC) of the Securities Commission.

Second Board Index
Second Board Index tracks the share price performance of all Second Board companies.

 

Release Schedule

KLCI has been discontinued. Data used to compile at monthly interval and used to be released at the beginning of the month.

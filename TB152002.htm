Indonesia: Foreign Trade Statistics (Indonesia Custom Fare 2012)

Source: Indonesia Statistic Bureau (BPS)

Background

Scope

Source of Data
Foreign trade consists of export and import on goods. All data compiled are based on documents of export-import declaration issued by Custom and Excise Offices. Data obtained are based on complete enumeration, and received from Custom and Excise Offices (Kantor Pelayanan Bea dan Cukai) located across the country.

Coverage
The export and import statistics shown in the monthly return do not include the following goods:

- Clothes and jewelry of passengers.
- Luggage of passengers of own use, except refrigerators, television sets, etc.
- Goods exported/imported for the use of countries representative (embassies).
- Goods for expeditions and exhibition shows.
- A part of the goods directly exported/imported by armed forces.
- Packing containers to be refilled.
- Bank notes and securities.
- Sample goods.

 

Methodology

Classification of goods
Data of export and import by commodity from January 2012 are compiled according to HS classification based on the reference on Indonesia Custom Fare 2012.

Data used in this classification is based on the Harmonized System (HS) and the Standard International Trade Classification (SITC) Revision 3.

Tariff post on this classification is 10,025.

 

Release Schedule

Data is released on the first working day every current month.

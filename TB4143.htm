Vietnam: Investment

Source: General Statistics Office

Background

Investment identifies the size, structure of investment capital by investment source, kinds of economic activity, investment item and territory to prepare plans for effective and efficient implementation and use of investment, and to have appropriate macro management policies, such as mobilization of capital, adjustment of investment structure by region and by investment sector and kinds of activity, etc in each period of development of the nation.

 

Definition(s)

Investment
Investment is the entire amount of expenses (spending) to maintain or increase production capacity and resources to improve the material and spiritual living standards of the entire society in a given period, including investment to generate fixed assets, investment to increase current assets, spending on purchasing precious assets, reserving gold in the form of goods, commodities and stocks in residents and other investment to improve people�s knowledge, enhance social welfare, improve the ecological environment, support people�s welfare, etc.

 

Scope

Three Economic Sectors:

By Vietnam Standard Industrial Classification 2007

Sector I: Agriculture, Forestry and Fishery.

Sector II: Includes Mining and Quarrying, Manufacturing, Electricity, Gas, Air Condition Supply, Water Supply, Sewerage, Remediation, Construction.

Sector III: Includes services besides Sector I and Sector II.

By Vietnam Standard Industrial Classification 1993

Sector I: Agriculture, Forestry and Fishery.

Sector II: Industry and Construction including mining and quarrying, manufacturing and production and supply of electricity, gas and water, construction.

Sector III: Services including services besides Sector I and Sector II.

 

Note

Investment: State (VOCHAA) refers to development investment capital from State Budget, Government bond, credits guaranteed/issued by the government and other State capital sources (fees, charges, financial reserve fund�).

State Investment: ytd (VOCHE) refers to investment capital from State Budget only.

 

Release Schedule

State Investment is published in monthly and annual basis. Investment is published in annual basis.
